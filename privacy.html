Privacy Policy – Potty Pal AI

Effective date: August 29, 2025

Who we are

Potty Pal AI is operated by Practical AI Innovations (Individual / Sole Proprietor).

Contact: support@pottypalai.com

This policy covers the Potty Pal AI mobile apps (iOS/Android) and our marketing website.

What we do

Potty Pal AI helps parents/caregivers potty train toddlers using an AI assistant, personalized plans, and simple progress logging. The app is for adults to use on behalf of their children.

Information we collect

  • Account: Parent/guardian name, email, password.
  • Child profile: First name or nickname only (no last name), date of birth, and gender/pronouns. We do not collect profile photos.
  • Activity: Potty events (successes, accidents, efforts, optional notes) and a short rolling AI chat history for context.
  • Technical: Device/OS/app version and basic diagnostics. No precise location tracking.
  • Payments (if you subscribe): We receive purchase status/product identifiers from Apple/Google via RevenueCat; we do not receive full card numbers.

How we use information

  • Provide core features (accounts, profiles, logs, personalized guidance).
  • Personalize age- and history-based tips.
  • Customer support and service communications.
  • Security/fraud prevention and to comply with law.
  • Improve app quality and reliability.

We do not sell personal data and we do not show ads to children.

AI data handling

Our assistant uses Google Gemini. We design prompts to avoid sending personally identifiable information about you or your child. Only the minimum context needed is used for responses.

Children’s privacy and parental consent

The app is for adults. Children cannot create accounts.

Parents may add limited child information to personalize coaching.

We require parental consent before collecting child data. We present clear disclosures in-app and record consent; you can withdraw consent at any time within the app.

Legal bases (EEA/UK)

Contract (to provide the app), consent (for child data), legitimate interests (safety/fraud/improvements), and legal obligations.

Sharing

We share only with providers that help operate the app, under strict agreements:

  • Supabase (hosting/database/auth; Row Level Security limits access so each user only sees their own data).
  • Google Gemini (AI responses; minimal context, no intentional PII).
  • Apple/Google and RevenueCat (subscription validation; receipts and product identifiers).
  • Email/support tools and website hosting/analytics (site only).

We may disclose information if required by law or during a merger/acquisition, with notice where legally permissible.

Security

Encryption in transit, access controls, least-privilege practices.

Row Level Security (RLS) so users can only access their own records.

No method is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your information and address issues promptly.

Data retention and deletion

Account, child profiles, potty logs, and AI messages are kept while your account is active.

When you delete your account in-app, associated personal and child data are deleted from active systems within 30 days, except where we must retain limited records to meet legal obligations (e.g., taxes/purchases). Backups purge on normal cycles.

We may retain de-identified, aggregate statistics.

Your choices and rights

Access, correct, or delete your information.

Withdraw consent (including parental consent) at any time; if withdrawn, we stop processing child data and may delete related records unless retention is legally required.

EEA/UK: object/restrict, portability, and complaint rights with your data protection authority.

Use in-app settings or contact support@pottypalai.com.

No medical advice

Potty Pal AI provides educational coaching and general guidance only. It is not medical advice and is not a substitute for professional care.

Changes

We will post updates in the app and on our website and update the effective date.

Providers (transparency)

  • Supabase (hosting/database/auth)
  • Google AI Studio / Gemini (AI)
  • Apple App Store / Google Play / RevenueCat (purchases)
  • Email/helpdesk and website hosting/analytics (site only)
